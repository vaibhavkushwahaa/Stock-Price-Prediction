Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for an account on the Stock Market Analysis platform?

To register for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your details, such as your username, email, and password, and follow the prompts to complete the registration process. Once registered, you can log in and start using the platform.

2. What types of stock data can I access on this platform?

Our platform provides access to a wide range of stock data, including current and historical stock prices, technical indicators, and market predictions. Additionally, you can access related news articles and analysis to stay informed about the latest market trends.

3. How does the stock prediction feature work?

The stock prediction feature uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze historical data and market trends. By leveraging Python's sklearn library, our models predict future stock prices and trends. These predictions can help users make informed investment decisions.

4. Can I customize my dashboard to show my favorite stocks and news?

Yes, you can customize your dashboard by marking stocks as favorites. Once marked, these stocks will appear in your personalized dashboard, along with relevant news articles and predictions. This feature allows you to quickly access information on the stocks that matter most to you.

5. How is my data protected on this platform?

We take data security seriously. Our platform uses robust encryption protocols to protect your personal information and stock data. Additionally, we follow best practices for data privacy and compliance with relevant regulations to ensure your data remains secure and confidential.
